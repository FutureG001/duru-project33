About Me

My name is Duru Chimaobi, a passionate computer science student based in Nigeria, driven by a strong desire to build, create, and make meaningful impact through technology and digital innovation. My journey is shaped by curiosity, resilience, and a deep commitment to continuous learning.

From an early stage, I developed a strong interest in how systems work—both in technology and in real life. This led me into the world of computer science, where I began exploring areas such as web development, APIs, and backend systems. I am particularly interested in building solutions that combine creativity with functionality, especially those that can scale and serve real-world needs.

Beyond academics, I am actively involved in digital content creation, with a focus on storytelling. I run and manage YouTube platforms where I create engaging, cinematic stories—primarily inspired by folktales. My storytelling style blends simplicity with depth, aiming to capture human emotions, moral lessons, and the complexity of character. I believe stories are one of the most powerful ways to connect with people, and I continuously work on improving how I present them visually and narratively.

My creative process involves generating visuals, voiceovers, and animations to produce immersive content that holds audience attention. Through this, I have developed skills in video editing, audience engagement, and content strategy—learning how to not just create, but also communicate effectively in a digital space.

In addition to content creation, I have a strong interest in building digital systems. One of my long-term goals is to develop a “story engine”—an intelligent platform powered by AI that can generate structured, high-quality stories at scale. This reflects my vision of combining technology and creativity into something impactful and innovative.

I am also deeply motivated by growth and independence. My journey includes exploring different paths to financial sustainability, learning from experience, and continuously adapting to new opportunities. I am committed to building a future where I can create value, help others, and achieve long-term success through discipline and consistency.

At my core, I am someone who believes in progress—learning every day, improving steadily, and turning ideas into reality.
